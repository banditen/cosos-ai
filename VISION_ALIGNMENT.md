## üéØ The Core Problem (from PROBLEM-SOLUTION.md)

**Founders spend too much time gathering information and figuring out what to do next.**

They become the "human glue" holding the organization together:
- Tracking down scattered data across tools
- Asking team members for updates
- Manually building reports to understand what's happening
- Making decisions in the dark without real-time context

**This is the "operational fog."**

---

## üí° The Long-Term Vision (from PROBLEM-SOLUTION.md)

**Build horizontal intelligence infrastructure that automates operational insight and decision-making.**

Not workflow automation (commoditized).  
Not task execution (race to zero).  
**Decision infrastructure** (compounds over time, becomes irreplaceable).

A system that:
1. Helps founders define goals and metrics properly
2. Constantly gathers and interprets data across tools
3. Proactively surfaces insights, risks, and blockers
4. Proposes or executes next actions based on context

Over time, it becomes:
- An AI COO
- An AI chief of staff  
- An AI operational brain

**This is infrastructure, not tooling.**

## üöÄ The MVP Entry Point (from STRATEGIC_PIVOT.md & BLUEPRINT.md)

**Problem with original approach:** Auto-generate an "Operating System Blueprint" from onboarding.
- Too vague without full company data
- Unclear utility ("Cool visualization... now what?")
- No immediate action

**New approach:** Prompt-driven artifact generation.

**"Tell me what you need, I'll build it for you."**

Examples:
- "Track our path to $100k MRR" ‚Üí MRR Growth Tracker
- "Figure out why activation dropped" ‚Üí Activation Funnel Monitor
- "Help me prepare for board meeting" ‚Üí Board Prep Package
- "We're struggling with retention" ‚Üí Retention Analysis System

**This is our Gamma moment:** One prompt ‚Üí Custom business system in 10 seconds.

## üõ§Ô∏è The Path from MVP to Vision

### Phase 1: Prompt-Driven Artifacts (MVP - Nov 2025)
**What we're building NOW:**
- 5-screen onboarding (Welcome ‚Üí Stage ‚Üí Goal ‚Üí Challenge ‚Üí Prompt)
- Prompt classification engine (LLM determines artifact type)
- Artifact generation service (creates structured content)
- Flexible templates (trackers, dashboards, analyses, frameworks)
- Database schema (artifacts, prompts, updates)

**Value delivered:**
- Immediate (10 seconds from prompt to artifact)
- User-driven (you tell us what you need)
- Flexible (different outputs for different needs)
- Works without integrations (smart templates and frameworks)

**Why this matters:**
- Proves we understand founder problems
- Delivers immediate value (no setup tax)
- Creates foundation for data collection
- Builds trust for deeper integration

---

### Phase 2: Connected Intelligence (Q1 2026)
**What we'll build:**
- Integration activation (Stripe, PostHog, Linear, Gmail/Calendar)
- Artifacts update with real data
- Basic monitoring and alerts
- Progressive enhancement (artifacts get better with data)

**Value delivered:**
- Artifacts become live (not static)
- Monitoring begins automatically
- Alerts when thresholds crossed
- Daily/weekly summaries of changes

**Why this matters:**
- Starts building the business intelligence graph
- Begins continuous data gathering
- Establishes monitoring patterns
- Learns what metrics matter to each user

---

### Phase 3: Proactive Intelligence (Q2-Q3 2026)
**What we'll build:**
- Autonomous daily/weekly briefs (without prompting)
- Predictive analytics (runway, growth, velocity)
- Market intelligence (competitor monitoring)
- Strategic scenario planning
- Proactive risk flagging

**Value delivered:**
- COSOS analyzes business every morning
- Surfaces insights before problems become crises
- Recommends strategic moves
- Connects actions to outcomes

**Why this matters:**
- Shifts from reactive (user asks) to proactive (COSOS tells)
- Demonstrates strategic reasoning capability
- Builds institutional memory
- Proves value of accumulated context

---

### Phase 4: Horizontal Infrastructure (Q4 2026 - 2027)
**What we'll build:**
- Multi-domain intelligence (sales, product, finance)
- Real-time business operating system
- Autonomous decision-making with guardrails
- Continuous learning and adaptation
- Cross-domain synthesis

**Value delivered:**
- COSOS understands entire business
- Coordinates decisions across domains
- Executes low-risk decisions automatically
- Recommends medium/high-risk decisions with reasoning
- Becomes institutional memory and strategic advisor

**Why this matters:**
- This IS the vision from PROBLEM-SOLUTION.md
- Decision infrastructure, not task automation
- Compounds over time, becomes irreplaceable
- Clients pay $5k-$20k/month, not $200/month
